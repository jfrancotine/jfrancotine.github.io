01 — Introduction

Bilingual software developer who builds things thoughtfully — and led teams across three countries before writing his first line of Java.

I hold a B.S. in Software Development from Bellevue University on top of a Bachelor of Business Administration from Central University of Venezuela. Over the last decade I've led teams in retail, run a supermarket in Chile, sold pharmaceuticals in Venezuela, and managed VIP banking clients — picking up English along the way and now applying that operational mindset to clean, well-tested code.

I work in Java, Python, PHP, JavaScript, and SQL. I care about Agile collaboration, version control hygiene, and code that someone else can read six months later. Open to relocation and travel.
