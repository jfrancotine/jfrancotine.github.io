About

A developer with an operator's mindset.

I hold a B.S. in Software Development from Bellevue University on top of a Bachelor of Business Administration from Central University of Venezuela. Three consecutive Dean's List awards with a 4.0 GPA, and member of Omega Nu Lambda National Honor Society.

I care about Agile collaboration, clean version control, and code that someone else can read six months from now. Currently building production WordPress sites for clients while looking for the right software development role.
